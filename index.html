Blood count

Heart checkup

Your heart health is crucial, and our specialized heart checkup services ensure a thorough evaluation of your cardiovascular system. Through advanced diagnostic techniques, including ECG, stress tests, and echocardiograms, we assess heart function, detect any abnormalities, and provide personalized care to keep your heart in optimal condition.

Urinalysis

Our precise urinalysis tests analyze the composition of your urine to provide insights into your kidney function, urinary tract health, and overall hydration status. Detecting any irregularities or infections early can aid in prompt treatment and preventive measures for better urinary health.

CT scan

With our cutting-edge CT scan technology, we capture detailed cross-sectional images of your body, aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions. Whether it's assessing injuries, detecting tumors, or evaluating internal organs, our CT scans deliver high-resolution images for accurate and timely diagnoses.

Cholesterol test

Keep tabs on your cholesterol levels to maintain a healthy heart. Our cholesterol tests measure the levels of different cholesterol components, including LDL, HDL, and triglycerides.
